WHO ARE WE ?

We are CHEEZY in the quest to provide you with the best FOOD in town.

We believed in a dream and we made it happen.What started as a small 40 sq ft stall in 2009, has now transformed to a full functioning cafe with multiple outlets already in Pune.

JOURNEY

Incepted by Tushar Kelkar, a food enthusiast had a vision to provide food that tingles Puneri taste-buds ( in the yummiest way possible).

FOOD AND HYGIENE

We live by our tradition and serve veg-only food, in the most hygienic and delicious way possible.
We take pride in making sure that our hygiene standards are at par with industry regulations. All our chefs, helpers and vendors from whom we source our materials have gone through careful screening to maintain good hygiene and all our food handlers are medically certified.

AMBIENCE

The ambience of our cafe is inspired by urban Pune life with hints of yellow lights and comfy seating for the young fam. Good music-good food and a place to hangout with close friends has made crazy cheesy a youngsters favourite space to chill.
In our quest to provide good quality pocket friendly food, we believe sky is the limit, and so we have geared ourselves up to expand our horizons to different localities within Pune and to cities beyond Pune.
